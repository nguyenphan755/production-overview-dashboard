Total mold changeover time:
in framas OEE system, the “Total mold changeover time” is defined as the duration between “Last good pair/piece” of current product/size and “First good pair/piece” of next product or size. This include
Mold Change
,

Mold change
: the operation for uninstalling the current mold, and installing the next mold.
Mold cleaning
: the operation for cleaning the installed mold. For continuous improvement of the total mold changeover time, effort shall be made to perform the “mold cleaning” before mold change.
Setting:
the operation for setting the injection molding parameter for the next product/size, making injection trial, until producing the first good pair/piece of product.
Key points:
Get everything prepared close to the machine before end of production of last batch, these may include: the next mold, tools, jigs, lifting hook, etc.
Using 2 persons to do the mold changeover if possible.
Person doing parameter setting shall start setting immediately after mold is installed onto the machine.

## Slide 13
Ideal Cycle Time (ICT)
How to determine the “Ideal Cycle Time” for existing products?
For existing products which are already complete the ramp-up phase, the ideal cycle time shall be determined by time measuring. A best experienced and skillful operator shall be selected for time study, who is fully qualified to perform the job.
Record the time for at least 10 cycles. Select the lowest consistently repeatable time. Do not use the average time since the lowest repeatable time is more indicative of what is realistically possible.
Pay attention to shop floor courtesy. Make clear that you are timing the work, not the operator.
